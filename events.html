Weekly & Monthly Events

Our recurring adventures — set these in your spell book.

Friday Night Magic

Every Friday from 6pm. Draft, Standard, Commander, and casual formats. Entry: $15 draft / $5 casual. Prizes every round for top finishers. We're a WPN Premium store — sanctioned events every week.

Every Friday 6pm Start
🎨

Painting Tutorials

Every 2nd & 4th Saturday at 2pm. Sessions run 2–3 hours. Beginner through advanced. Materials provided for novices. Bring your own minis or pick one up in-store.

Bi-weekly Sat 2pm Start
🍻

Pint Night

Every Thursday at 7pm. New seasonal ale revealed each month. Themed trivia, casual gaming, and drink specials. Tables available — no booking needed.

Every Thursday 7pm Start
🐉

Campaign Night

Open TTRPG gaming every Wednesday from 6pm. Find a group, join an existing campaign, or run your own. All systems welcome — D&D, Pathfinder, Call of Cthulhu, and more.

Every Wednesday 6pm Start
🏆

Monthly Tournament

First Sunday of each month — grand tournament event rotating between TCG systems, board games, and TTRPG one-shots. Prizes, glory, and store credit await.
